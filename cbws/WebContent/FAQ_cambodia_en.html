• General
    • Q1
      • What is Hong Leong Connect?
        • Hong Leong Connect brings you a host of digital banking services offered by Hong Leong Bank Vietnam (HLBVN) to our customers. At present, Hong Leong Connect offers Online Banking on personal computer terminals or any electronic device through the Internet browser and Mobile Banking application on 2 smart phone platforms - iOS and Android.
      Q2
      • What is Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking?
        • Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking allows customers on-the-move to access their bank accounts and to perform online banking transactions via a dedicated application on their smart phones (iOS or Android).
      Q3
      • Who is eligible for Hong Leong Connect Online and Mobile Banking?
        • All Hong Leong Bank customers with an active ATM/Debit Card or Account are eligible to register for Hong Leong Connect Online and Mobile Banking.

      Q6
      • What is an Activation Code?
        • An Activation Code is a list of alphanumeric characters issued to you by the Bank upon your request via Hong Leong Call Centre or at Branch. This code can only be used once and is valid for 3 days upon creation.
      Q7
      • How do I obtain an Activation Code?
        • You can obtain an Activation Code at any Branch/Transaction Office of Hong Leong Bank or contact our Hong Leong Call Centre at + 84 8 73008100
      Q8
      • What is a TAC?
        • Transaction Authorisation Code - TAC is a 6-digit unique security code that is used for specific online banking transactions. The TAC is valid for 5 minutes upon request.
      Q9
      • How do I apply for the TAC?
        • It is important that you must first register your mobile phone number at any Hong Leong Banks' Branch/Transaction Office or via Hong Leong Call Centre. Subsequently, when you are performing specific transaction via Hong Leong Connect, system will automatically send a TAC to your registered mobile phone number.

  • Pay and Transact
    • Q1.
      • Will there be any record for my safekeeping when I perform a fund transfer via Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking?
        • You will receive an acknowledgment for any financial transaction performed via Mobile Banking which will have a reference number. This page can be printed by clicking the "Print" button. You may also retrieve your online transaction records in “View History” menu.
      Q2.
      • Can I perform fund transfer to any account via Mobile Banking?
        • You can transfer to any account at HLBVN or other banks in Vietnam. Currently, our Mobile Banking does not support transferring to the beneficiary accounts which are opened outside Vietnam. Once we launch this function, HLBVN will notify you through the official channels of the Bank.
      Q3.
      • What are the differences between “Instant Transfer” and “Normal Transfer”?
        • Instant Transfer is inter-bank transfer service (IBT) through Mobile Banking provided by HLBVN is in collaboration with SmartLink Card Services Joint Stock Company (SmartLink) to increase the utility for cardholders. This service allows ATM/Debit Cardholders of HLBVN to make instant money transfers from their card to another ATM/Debit Card or Current/Savings account of other banks in SmartLink network.

          Normal Transfer is inter-bank transfer service through Mobile Banking provided by HLBVN via payment gateway of The State Bank of Vietnam. This service allows account holders of HLBVN to make money transfers from their account to any account of other banks in Vietnam.

          “Transfer Type” whether it is Normal Transfer or Instant Transfer will be determined by:
          Field
          Normal Transfer
          Instant Transfer (SmartLink)
          From Account
          Current and Savings

          Currency
          Supports VND and FCY
          Supports VND only
          Recipient Bank

          For banks that support IBT via SmartLink network
          Recipient Account Type
          Only supports :

          • Current/Savings Account

          • Credit Card

          • Loan Account

          • Others

          Only supports :

          • Personal Current/Savings Account (only if recipient bank supports CASA transfer)

          • ATM/Debit Card

          * FCY = Foreign Currency Account

        • Your transaction will be unsuccessful.
      Q12.

        • Yes, you may perform the 3rd Party Fund Transfer by selecting "Pay & Transact" – "Transfer to Other accounts at HLBVN" for Savings and Current accounts.
      Q13.
      • After I have performed a payment or transfer, when will the fund be transferred?
        • The funds will be transferred immediately when the transaction has been performed successfully.

  • About PEx
    • Q1.
      • What is PEx?
        • PEx is short for Payment Express, a revolutionary new mobile-to-mobile money transfer service. PEx enables you to pay from your Hong Leong Current or Savings account to any local mobile number on your smart phone contacts address book or to anyone with a local mobile number. Payee is required to collect the PEx payment via Internet to transfer the fund into his/her HLBVN account.
      Q2.
      • How can the person receive the PEx payment from me?
        • The payee will first receive an SMS from Hong Leong Bank to notify them of the PEx payment performed by you. The notification SMS contains the payment amount and advise him/her to contact sender for payment collection within 3 days of the transaction date. The payee would need to contact you for the Collection Code before they can collect the PEx payment successfully. It is your responsibility to verify the payee and to disclose the Collection Code to the payee only and not to anybody else.
      Q3.
      • How come there is no PEx Collection in my Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking app?
        • The PEx Collection option is only available at Hong Leong Connect Online Banking. Please login to the webpage at www.hongleongconnect.com.vn to receive your PEx payment.
      Q4.
      • Does the payee have to become a Hong Leong Connect Online Banking user in order to collect the PEx payment?
        • No. As long as the payee has a current/savings account at HLBVN. He/she will be able to make the said collection.
      Q5.
      • Is there a limit on the amount that can be transferred via PEx?
        • Yes. The default limit set by the Bank is VND 10,000,000 per day, but you can reset the limit to any amount up to VND 100,000,000 per day as you wish.
      Q6.
      • Where and how do I set the transaction limit for PEx?
        • Step 1: Login to Hong Leong Connect Online Banking account and Select "Other Services" and "Change Online Transaction Limit";
          Step 2: Enter your preferred limit and "Submit";
          Step 3: Enter the TAC number that you receive via mobile phone and "Submit". Select "Change Online Transaction Limit" again and you will see that your new transaction limit.
      Q7.
      • Do I need to register the payee's account in my Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking account for them to make the PEx payment collection?
        • No. As long as the payee has a Current/Savings account at HLBVN, he/she will be able to make the said collection.
      Q8.
      • What type of accounts can I use to make a PEx transaction?
        • You can use either your Hong Leong Current or Savings account to make a transfer payment via PEx.
      Q9.
      • Is there a service charge imposed for sending money via PEx?
        • No. However, do note that if you have opted to send a Collection Code to a payee via SMS, then you will be charged the standard SMS rate by your telco network.
      Q10.
      • Is there a service charge imposed for collecting a PEx payment?
        • No.
      Q11.
      • How do I know if the payee has collected the PEx payment?
        • Step 1:Login to your Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking account.
          Step 2:Select "PEx" on the menu;
          Step 3:Select "Transaction Status" on "PEx - Payment Express";
          Step 4:a)Select "Pending for collection" button to view a list of payments that have yet to be collected; or
          b)Select "Successful" button to view list of payments successfully collected.
      Q12.
      • Why is my payee unable to make a collection at the PEx Collection on Online Banking website?
        • There could be a few possibilities: -
          a)The payee may have entered unmatched/incorrect transaction data.
          b)The payee may have exceeded the maximum attempts allowed for entering the correct transaction data.
          c)The Collection Code has expired.
          d)The payer may have canceled the PEx Payment - please refer to payer.
      Q13.
      • My payee is unable to make the PEx payment collection as the Collection Code has expired. What should I do?
        • Step 1:Login to your Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking account;
          Step 2:Select "PEx" on the bottom menu;
          Step 3:Select "Transaction Status" on PEx - Payment Express;
          Step 4:Select "Failed" button to view the list of payment;
          Step 5:Once you have located the said expired payment in this list, you may proceed to re-initiate a PEx transaction to the said payee.
      Q14.
      • My payee has successfully collected the PEx payment once. Can he/she collect the same payment again?
        • No. The payee can collect a PEx payment one time only.
      Q15.
      • Can I cancel a PEx transaction that has been previously successfully initiated?
        • Yes, as long as it has not been collected by the payee. You are required to inform the payee prior cancellation of the PEx transaction.
      Q16.
      • What should I do if my smart phone ran out of battery before I was able to complete my PEx transaction?
        • Step 1:Login to your Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking account.
          Step 2:Select "PEx" on the bottom menu;
          Step 3:Select "Transaction Status" on PEx - Payment Express;
          Step 4:Select "Pending for collection" button. If you do not find your transaction here, you may proceed to initiate the PEx transaction again.
      Q17.
      • Can I send the Collection Code via SMS to an overseas mobile number?
        • No. At the moment Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking only accepts Vietnamese mobile phone numbers.

  • PEx+
    • Q1.
      • What is PEx+?
        • PEx+ is a service which allows Hong Leong Connect customer to use their mobile device to make purchases at participating Merchants (included its location, office, unit and/or place of business providing goods and services) that registered with the Bank on the acceptance of PEx+ as a mean of payment.
      Q2.
      • Who are the Merchants which accept PEx+ payment?
        • The Merchants are listed on the Merchant Directory on your Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking landing page. After launch the app, select Merchant Directory. The Merchants are listed there. You can tap on the left most icon on the Merchant Details page to find the location of the Merchant.
      Q3.
      • What do I need to be able to pay using PEx+?
        • You must:-
          •have a HLBVN Current or Saving Account in VND
          •have either an Apple or Android mobile device with a camera
          •download the Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking app from the Apple AppStore/Google Playstore
          •register as a Hong Leong Connect customers
      Q4.
      • What is the maximum purchase amount that I can make?
        • In one day your total PEx+ purchases cannot exceed the PEx+ Transaction Limit as posted in our Online Banking.
      Q5.
      • Is there a minimum purchase amount?
        • No. We will inform all customers if there is any updates.
      Q6.
      • Will I be charged for using PEx+?
        • Currently, you do not have to pay any service charge for using PEx+.
      Q7.
      • Upon making a PEx+ transaction, will the amount be deducted instantly from my account?
        • Yes, the amount will be deducted instantly from your account.
      Q8.
      • Can I cancel the PEx+ transaction that has been initiated successfully?
        • No, the PEx+ transaction cannot be cancelled.
      Q9.
      • Can I return the goods which I purchased?
        • Return of goods purchased will follow the Merchant’s return policy.
      Q10.
      • I have returned the goods to the Merchant and the Merchant confirms that he has reversed out the PEx+ sales. How can I check that he has done so?
        • When the Merchant reverse out the PEx+ sale, you should receive an SMS informing you that the reversal has been done. You can also check the sales reversal by:-
          i)Login to Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking.
          ii)Go to Account Overview
          iii)Select the Account which you used and look for the reversal transaction. The description of the transaction should be one of the following:-
          • MB PEx+ Sales Reversal CA to CA
          • MB PEx+ Sales Reversal CA to SA
          • MB PEx+ Sales Reversal SA to CA
          • MB PEx+ Sales Reversal SA to SA
      Q11.
      • Can I give my handphone to the Cashier to scan the QR Barcode?
        • No. You should never give your handphone to the Cashier to scan the barcode or to enter any information required to complete the purchase.
      Q12.
      • How can I get a copy of the payment receipt made using PEx+?
        • Yes. At the bottom of the acknowledgement page, slide to “Receipt” and enter your email address
      Q13.
      • How secure is this payment method?
        • This payment method of our bank is secure as a PEx+ payment can only be made:-
          • from a mobile device which has been tagged/bound to your Hong Leong Connect Mobile Banking app
          • requires your Hong Leong Connect Password
      Q14.
      • I have registered as a Hong Leong Connect customer. However, when I tap on the PEx+ icon it tells me that my mobile device is not tagged. What should I do?
        • You are using a mobile device which you have not tagged to your Hong Leong Connect Account. On the same device, login to Hong Leong Connect, you will be asked to tag your mobile device.
      Q15.
      • I tapped on the PEx+ icon and entered my Password. I could not log in as the system kept prompting that my Password is not valid. I am very sure that I entered the correct password?
        • Are you using your own device (i.e. device which has been tagged to your account) or a device tagged to another Hong Leong Connect user? If you are using a device tagged to another Hong Leong Connect user, you will not able to use the PEx+ function.
      Q16.
      • The Acknowledgement Page was displayed and the status of the PEx+ transaction was “Successful”. The Merchant however said that he did not receive acknowledgement that the transaction occurred? How can I check whether my account balance was deducted?
        • Login to Hong Leong Connect. Go to Account Services. Select the Account which you used and check on the date when you carried out the transaction, whether the PEx+ transaction is listed. The description of the transaction should be one of the following:-
          • MB PEx+ Sales CA to CA
          • MB PEx+ Sales CA to SA
          • MB PEx+ Sales SA to CA
          • MB PEx+ Sales SA to SA
      Q17.
      • The Acknowledgement Page was displayed and the status of the PEx+ transaction was “Successful”. The Merchant however said that he did not receive acknowledgement that the transaction occurred. I have checked my account information and the PEx+ transaction is shown. How do I get my money back?
        • This scenario could occur if the Merchant Terminal does not receive the Acknowledgement Page due to the internet connection. Should the Customer deal directly with the Merchant or should HLBVN mediate between the Merchant and the Customer to determine whether the Customer’s complaint is genuine.
      Q18.
      • No Acknowledgement Page was displayed but when I check my account balance, the funds was deducted?
        • This scenario could occur if the internet connection at both the Merchant Terminal and Customer Phone dropped before the Acknowledgement Page was displayed. Please advise Merchant to retrieve this transaction in the Terminal. Should the Customer deal directly with the Merchant or should HLBVN mediate between the Merchant and the Customer to determine whether the Customer’s complaint is genuine.
